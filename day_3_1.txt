Given: Family has 2 children

let b represent boy and g represent girl respectively.

possibilities: bb,bg,gb,gg (4)

but,

Given: one child is a boy (b)

hence,

remaining possibilities: bb,bg,gb (3) 

(gg is discarded as both can't be girls)

thus,

let E represent the event that both the children are boys given one child is a boy.

P(E): 1/3

Answer is 1/3.
